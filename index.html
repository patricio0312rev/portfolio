NEKO: Proposal of the first super-agile methodology to improve work efficiency

Abstract

Agile methodologies are the preferred strategy for work teams around the world thanks to their rapid response to changes and adaptability. However, this prioritization of efficiency and speed has neglected the well-being of the most important element of any project: the teams. This article presents NEKO as a super-agile methodology focused on people and that aims to prioritize the mental health of workers without neglecting effectiveness, relying on new technologies such as Artificial Intelligence to enhance its processes. During the research stage, using the methodology proposed by Kitchenham, fifteen articles were analyzed and allowed us to rescue the strengths and identify the problems presented by the agile methodologies most used today. Then, the proposal was developed dividing it into three phases; each including events, artifacts, and tools to achieve your goal. Later, during the discussion, the proposal was contrasted with the methodologies initially analyzed and it was concluded that taking the points in favor of these and adding state-of-the-art technology focused on the well-being of the teams is synonymous with efficiency, meaning a possible change in the paradigm of the current world of work.

Keywords

agile project management, methodology, mental health, work teams, effectiveness

2022

Quality Evaluation Model based on ISO/IEC25010 for Cloud E-learning Platforms andbased on Laravel

Abstract

Cloud computing has become the standard for all software architecture developed today. This is reflected in the more than 1,191 trillion dollars that this industry has generated in 2021. However, there are still some challenges that this paradigm must face and respondto. The objective of this article is to propose a five-layer architecture using AWS services, seeking to respond to the challenges of security, information co-location,and availability onhigh-demand. To prepare the proposal, a review of the literature was carried out consisting of 12 scientific articles, which were taken as antecedents and from which the strengths or innovative aspects were rescued. Later, the proposal was developed consideringthe analyzed antecedents and the experience of each member of the research team; developing a proposal that uses services like EC2, RDS, Route53, S3 storage,and load balancers. The architecture model was graphed, explained,and applied in a case study that resulted in the efficiency of this architecture in an e-learning system based on the Laravel framework and that receives thousands of users from different parts of the world.

Keywords

software architecture, cloud computing, Laravel framework, AWS

2022

Quality Evaluation Model based on ISO/IEC25010 for Cloud E-learning Platforms andbased on Laravel

Abstract

Software development has become one of the most im-portant industries in the world today, valued at $ 968.25trillion in 2021. However, as this industry grows, therisks of making mistakes that could lead to the failureof major projects also increase. There are internationalstandards that are dedicated to evaluating the qualityof software, but they are difficult to apply and to beobjective with their evaluation. The objective of this ar-ticle is to propose a quality evaluation model based onISO 25010 that allows objectively measuring the qualityof an e-learning system in the cloud and based on Lar-avel. After having analyzed 16 articles as background,the evaluation model was proposed, divided into a partevaluated by the users of the platform and the other car-ried out by third-party software, each with criteria andmetrics to be evaluated. Later, this model was tested ina case study on a platform that obtained a score of 88.5as a quality index, which was interpreted as approvedwithin the scale proposed in the model. In conclusion,it was obtained that it is possible to be objective whenmeasuring the quality of a software when applying thismodel and that this model could be applied in softwarefocused on other sectors of the industry.

Keywords

software quality, quality evaluation model,Laravel, cloud computing, e-learning
